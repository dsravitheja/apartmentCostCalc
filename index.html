ℹ️Important Notes & Caveats
  • Perquisite per IT Rule 3(2): ≤1600cc = ₹1,800/mo · >1600cc = ₹2,400/mo · company driver = +₹900/mo.
  • Old regime includes standard deduction (₹50,000) + 80C (₹1.5L) only. HRA, 80D excluded for simplicity.
  • New regime (FY2025-26): standard deduction ₹75,000; rebate u/s 87A for taxable ≤ ₹12L.
  • Carve-out (A): your CTC reduces by car package; employer pays EMI/fuel/driver. Your take-home reduces but no car expense.
  • Additive (B): car is on top of your CTC; full salary retained; only perquisite value added to taxable income.
  • Consult a CA for personalised tax planning before restructuring your salary.
